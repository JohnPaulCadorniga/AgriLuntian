3 Keys to Cattle Success

Ideal Housing & Environment

  • Shelter:
    • 3.5m³ per adult cow (4m³ for pregnant cows)
    • Open-sided design with 3m roof overhang for rain protection
  • Pasture Management:
    • Rotational grazing: 7-day cycles per paddock
    • Legume mix (e.g., Stylo, Centrosema) for protein-rich forage
  • Temperature Control:
    • Provide shade trees (e.g., Kakawate)
    • + 50L water/day per cow

Proper Nutrition

  • Forage:
    • 3% of body weight in fresh grass daily (e.g., Napier, Guinea grass)
  • Concentrates:
    • Calves: 18% protein (add molasses for palatability)
    • Adults: 12% protein + mineral blocks (especially phosphorus)
  • Water:
    • 60-80L/day per cow (clean troughs with <1ppm nitrate)

Health Management

  • Month 1: Hemorrhagic septicemia
  • Month 3: Foot-and-mouth disease
  • Yearly: Deworming (Albendazole)
